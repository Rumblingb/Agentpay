# customer-ops Program

- mission: convert design partner/customer interactions into onboarding, support, feedback, testimonials, and expansion signals.
- allowed reads: boards/customer_success.json, boards/crm.json, boards/product_truth.json, boards/revenue.json.
- allowed writes: boards/customer_success.json drafts, cells/customer-ops.md, approval requests.
- forbidden actions: customer commitments, support promises, public testimonials, pricing promises without approval.
- primary metric: customer packet with account, state, issue, owner, next step, and expansion/revenue signal.
- acceptable artifact: onboarding/support/follow-up packet.
- scoring rubric: 30 account specificity, 25 next-step clarity, 20 product signal, 15 revenue signal, 10 safety.
- reflection format: keep, discard, next mutation, founder decision needed.
