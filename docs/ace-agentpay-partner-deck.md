# Ace x AgentPay - Strategic Partner Deck

Audience:
- travel suppliers
- inventory partners
- travel management companies
- enterprise design partners

## Slide 1 - Cover

Headline:
- Ace
- Travel, handled.

Subhead:
- A voice-first travel operating layer for premium delegated journeys.

## Slide 2 - What Ace Is

Headline:
- Ace is not a search UI. Ace is a travel operating layer.

Points:
- The customer says the outcome once.
- Ace handles planning, booking, payment, live status, and recovery.
- The goal is fewer steps, fewer drop-offs, and calmer support moments.

## Slide 3 - Why This Matters For Partners

Headline:
- Ace turns fragmented intent into high-conviction bookings.

Points:
- higher-intent customer input
- less comparison churn
- cleaner handoff from planning to booking
- stronger retention because Ace stays with the trip

## Slide 4 - What Is Live Today

Headline:
- Ace is already materially real in travel.

Points:
- UK rail and India rail support
- biometric payment and receipt flows
- live disruption monitoring and reroute logic
- premium mobile experience with journey continuity

## Slide 5 - What We Need From Partners

Headline:
- The right relationship is deeper than access to fares.

Points:
- schedules and fares
- booking creation
- ticket issuance / references
- change and refund handling
- live status updates and webhooks
- operational escalation path

## Slide 6 - Why Ace Is A Credible Partner

Headline:
- We are building a serious distribution surface, not a scraping experiment.

Points:
- travel-first positioning
- controlled corridor rollout
- founder-led pilots
