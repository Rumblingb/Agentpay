# AgentPay Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

## Purpose
This policy is designed to prevent abuse of AgentPay's settlement verification endpoints and protect the integrity of the AgentPay ecosystem.

## Prohibited Activities
- Automated scraping, denial-of-service, or excessive polling of API endpoints
- Fraudulent, deceptive, or malicious use of AgentPay services
- Attempts to circumvent rate limits, authentication, or security controls
- Use of AgentPay for illegal, unethical, or harmful purposes

## Enforcement
Violations may result in suspension or termination of access, and reporting to relevant authorities.

## Contact
For questions or to report abuse, contact: legal@agentpay.gg
