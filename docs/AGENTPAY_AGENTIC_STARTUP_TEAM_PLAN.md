# AgentPay Agentic Startup Team Plan

## North Star

AgentPay is the authority, identity, wallet, approval, billing, and payment layer for agents that act in the real world.

The five-year direction can include a physical personal AI companion with a name, inbox, wallet, multilingual voice, and agent-swarm control. The company-building system should work toward that future without letting hardware distract from the current wedge.

## Lane Boundary

Bill is separate.

Bill belongs to Hedge and owns trading, prediction markets, paper/live gates, risk, and market execution. Agency OS may surface Bill status to the founder, but it must not direct Bill's trading logic or risk posture.

Agency OS owns AgentPay Labs company execution:

- product proof
- outbound
- marketing
- advertising
- partnerships
- customer operations
- revenue operations
- collection and payment follow-up
- founder-ready approval packets

## Startup Team

Agency OS should operate like a small world-class startup team:

- Chief-of-staff: chooses the daily bottleneck and gives the founder one approval queue.
- Offer strategy: wedge, ICP, positioning, proof, and pricing.
- Build ops: repo truth, shipping handoffs, tests, demos, and reliability.
- Outbound ops: reachable prospects, send packets, follow-ups, and CRM hygiene.
- Media ops: proof-led demos, posts, landing-page copy, and launch assets.
- Revenue ops: pricing, payment links, invoices, collection follow-up, trial-to-paid, renewals, and cash movement.
- Ads ops: no-spend experiment packets until proof and budget are approved.
- Partnerships ops: provider, host, API-company, and channel partner packets.
- Customer ops: onboarding, support, feedback, testimonial asks, churn risk, and expansion signals.
- Audit layer: Hermes/OpenClaw watchdogs for stale cells, broken tools, missed follow-ups, and unsafe actions.

## Required Boards

The runtime needs hard state, not just prose:

- `crm.json`: lead, contact path, fit, source, next step, status, last touch, follow-up date.
- `send_queue.json`: recipient, channel, subject, body, approval status, follow-up date.
- `revenue.json`: account, amount, payment path, due date, status, owner, next action.
- `customer_success.json`: account, issue, status, feedback, testimonial/expansion signal.
- `content_queue.json`: asset, proof input, channel, draft, approval status, publish date.
- `ads_experiments.json`: campaign, target, creative, spend cap, stop rule, learning goal, result.
- `partnerships.json`: partner, decision-maker path, ask, proof asset, status, next step.
- `product_truth.json`: repo/demo/product truth and proof.
- `approvals.json`: founder approval queue for irreversible or external actions.
- `pnl.json`: revenue, costs, runway-impacting commitments, and reinvestment gates.
- `platform_submissions.json`: OpenAI, Claude, MCP directory, showcase, and partner submissions.
- `research_backlog.json`: research assignments required for the team to scale.

## Approval Rules

Autonomous by default:

- research
- drafting
- qualification
- board updates
- follow-up preparation
- internal handoffs
- proof packaging

Founder approval required:

- external sends
- public posts
- ad spend
- discounts/refunds
- pricing changes
- legal/compliance claims
- money movement
- partnership commitments
- customer promises beyond current product truth
