The Gap
AI can understand intent. It still cannot safely act.
Most products stop at recommendation. They do not know who the agent is, what it is allowed to do, how approval works, how payment is governed, or how completion is verified after money moves.
Understanding
Models can interpret nuanced requests in natural language.
Permission
No reliable identity, delegated limits, or approval chain.
Execution
No clean way to reserve budget, pay, and own the workflow.
Verification
No evidence trail, receipt spine, or auditable completion state.
Trust
When something goes wrong, nobody knows who is accountable.
AgentPay exists in that gap. It turns agents from suggestion engines into controlled, attributable, and verifiable actors.
