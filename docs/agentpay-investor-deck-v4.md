## Slide 2 - The Gap

### AI can understand intent. It still cannot safely act.

Core points:
- Most products stop at recommendation.
- They do not know who the agent is.
- They do not know what it is allowed to do.
- They do not know how approval works.
- They do not know how payment is governed.
- They do not know how completion is verified after money moves.

Close:

- It turns agents from suggestion engines into controlled, attributable, and verifiable actors.

## Slide 3 - The Modules

### Five modules turn agents from chat into infrastructure.

1. Identity
   - Verifiable agent identity and clear execution boundaries.
2. Policy
   - Limits, approvals, biometric gates, and escalation paths.
3. Execution
   - Intents, escrow, settlement, and controlled commercial flow.
4. Verification
   - Receipts, proof, recoverable states, and auditability.
5. Trust
   - Trust events, reputation, and compounding routing advantage.

Core line:
- The point of the company is making these modules reusable across every workflow where an agent needs to book, pay, verify, or be trusted.
